About NourishQuest

NourishQuest is a free and engaging nutrition planner built to help you reach your dietary goals without sacrificing fun. Our reward-oriented features are designed to keep you motivated and committed to your plan.

Our Mission

We want to make healthy eating accessible to everyone by removing subscription barriers and gamifying the nutrition experience.

What Makes Us Different

  • Completely Free — No monthly or annual fees
  • Fun Achievements — Stay motivated with badges and rewards
  • Personalized Plans — Curated diet plans for everyone
  • Community-Driven — We aim to build a friendly and supportive community
